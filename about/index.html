The Short Version

Trying to make philosophy a little more accessible.

The Long Version

I've always found papers in philosophy to hit that sweetspot between too long and too short. They usually aim at one small but substantial point that makes you go "Oh, I never thought about it that way!" For me, this makes philosophy papers prime content for sharing with others, and I always try to bring it up in interesting ways to friends. This is my attempt to recreate that magic from those conversations. Just like how PEL tries to recreate those conversations in the pub after a seminar, these posts try to recreate the conversations I've had after finishing a paper for one of my classes. I try to pick papers that (i) I think others will find interesting and (ii) I think makes a point that you're better off knowing.
